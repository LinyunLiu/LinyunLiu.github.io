# Summary
A smart college student named Pink has profound emotional distress, he is suffering from anxiety and depression. His emotional turmoil has intensified following a recent breakup with his girlfriend, Anna, leading to an escalating sense of unhappiness. It has dawned on him that, with his knowledge of computers, he can create a machine-learning AI that predicts the future according to the decisions a person will make. Pink wants to construct his own bright future. Soon he will have to learn the true meaning of life, and the only way for him to change his future that he has never discovered. 

Director LINYUN LIU 
Starring LETONG CHEN
